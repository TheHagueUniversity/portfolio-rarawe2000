Fashion Video (Make it Fashion - Call it Art)

This is a project I did in my free time, because I wanted to start working with video editing tools and combine music, fashion and videography in one project.

The idea behind the video is to encourage people to be creative and to be confident in going their own way. I made the video with minimalistic means, like old clothes I had left at home, and I recorded and edited it using my smartphone. I wanted to show that you do not have to be an expert with video editing tools, or you do not need a lot of equipment to be creative and to “make it fashion”.

Everyone can be creative; the point is to just get started and to have the courage to show that to the world. “We ain’t born typical” is a reference to being unique and to embracing individuality. Thinking outside the box and not following everyone else is what makes ideas stand out.
